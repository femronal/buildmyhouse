## Indexability summary (homeowner web)

Indexed: /, /for-contractors, tab SEO paths, construction/renovation/diaspora/guides/tools/downloads/demo/services/articles routes.

Noindex: auth flows, app dashboards, legacy redirects, private project routes.

robots.txt Disallow hints: /dashboard, /timeline, /stage-detail, /chat, /profile, /notifications, /pending-projects, /billing-payments, /house-summary, /upload-plan, etc.
