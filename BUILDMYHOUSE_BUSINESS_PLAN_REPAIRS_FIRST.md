# BuildMyHouse Business Plan (Repairs-First Growth)

## Executive Summary

BuildMyHouse is a remote property project management platform for Nigerians abroad. It helps diaspora homeowners manage property work in Nigeria with more structure, visibility, and control — starting with small repair and upgrade jobs, then expanding into renovation, interior upgrades, and full construction projects.

The core insight is simple: diaspora property owners do not mainly suffer from a lack of contractors. They suffer from a lack of trusted project control from a distance. Their real pain is unclear scope, weak oversight, payment pressure, poor updates, informal communication, and uncertainty about whether work is actually progressing as claimed.

BuildMyHouse's wedge is not "find a builder." It is "remotely manage property work in Nigeria with verified execution, stage-based control, and better visibility." The company's long-term category is broader than construction: it is a property work operating system for diaspora Nigerians.

Today, BuildMyHouse already has meaningful product foundations: a homeowner app, contractor app, admin dashboard, backend API, project and stage models, project requests, contractor verification, chat, notifications, and payment flows.

## Company Vision

To become the most trusted platform for Nigerians abroad to remotely manage any property work in Nigeria — from repairs to renovations to full builds.

## Mission

To replace chaotic, trust-heavy, informal property execution with clear scope, verified contractors, stage-based workflows, and evidence-backed progress.

## Product Guardrails

1. **Repairs-first focus:** Prioritize small, scoped repair/upgrade jobs before expanding into heavy renovation or full builds.
2. **Trust is operational, not decorative:** Every key flow must produce visible proof (scope clarity, stage status, evidence, payment logic).
3. **Stages before spending:** No feature should encourage money moving ahead of stage completion and verification.
4. **Proof over personality:** Contractor trust signals must come from verifiable records and process behavior, not smooth talk or referrals alone.
5. **Diaspora-first clarity:** UX copy and workflows must be understandable for Nigerians abroad managing projects remotely, not only for on-ground operators.
6. **Software + operations together:** BuildMyHouse is not a passive marketplace; product decisions must support review, escalation, and admin oversight.
7. **Narrow before broad:** Prefer deep reliability in one wedge over shallow breadth across many categories.
8. **Every update must reduce chaos:** If a feature does not improve control, visibility, or accountability, it is not a priority.

## Problem Statement

Diaspora Nigerians frequently invest in family homes, inherited property, rentals, and new housing projects in Nigeria. But execution from abroad is difficult because the project is usually managed through an unreliable mix of:

- family members
- informal referrals
- WhatsApp updates
- ad hoc site visits
- emotional payment pressure
- weak accountability

This leads to recurring failures:

- scope drift
- confusion between urgent repairs and optional upgrades
- money moving ahead of stage completion
- poor communication
- lack of documentation
- weak contractor accountability
- low confidence in what is actually happening on site

The underlying customer problem is not just "I need labor." It is:  
"I cannot confidently manage property work in Nigeria from abroad."

## Market Opportunity

- diaspora remittance-driven housing investment

## Risks

1. Trust risk  
   Software alone may not overcome offline trust habits.

2. Supply quality risk  
   Poor contractor quality can destroy trust quickly.

3. Positioning blur  
   Trying to do everything at once can weaken the value proposition.

4. Operational heaviness  
   This is not a pure SaaS model.

5. Overbuilding risk  
   Building too much breadth before one sharp wedge is proven.

## Risk Mitigation

- start with one narrow customer and one narrow job category
- keep early contractor supply curated
- use admin as a trust-control layer
- publish visible proof assets
- make stage and payment rules legible
- document operations early
- earn expansion rights through execution

## Funding / Capital Logic
