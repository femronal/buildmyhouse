# BuildMyHouse Content Workflow (Domain-first + Medium)

## Objective

Grow organic visibility while keeping SEO authority on `buildmyhouse.app`.

## Publishing model

1. Publish original article/page on BuildMyHouse domain.
2. Wait for indexation signal:
   - URL appears in Search Console index coverage, or
   - at least 24-72 hours after publish.
3. Republish an adapted version on Medium.
4. Ensure Medium post uses canonical link to original BuildMyHouse URL.
5. Add CTA links from Medium back to BuildMyHouse service/city/diaspora pages.

## Monthly cadence (capacity: 2-4 pieces)

- 2 service or city pages (high commercial intent)
- 1 diaspora-focused guide
- 1 supporting real-estate page (rent/sale/land)

## Quality checklist before publish

- One clear primary keyword + semantic variants
- Unique title, description, H1
- Internal links to at least 3 relevant indexed pages
- FAQ section for long-tail capture
- Strong CTA aligned to intent
- No duplicate full-text version published without canonical
